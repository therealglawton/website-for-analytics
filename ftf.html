Sorting

Fill The Fridge is a puzzle/casual sorting game where you organize your fridge after a supermarket shopping spree. Stock shelves with various items, finding the perfect spots for each. Enjoy the challenge of emptying your shopping carts and refilling the fridge. This engaging puzzle game makes organizing the kitchen both fun and satisfying.

Controls

  • Select and place items using the left mouse button
  • Activate Undo using the right mouse button
  • Confirm the sequence using the Space button
